About Us

We are a community organization from Itsoseng that teaches kids how to read. We began this drive back in 2020 with an aim of helping our community breach the educational gap by assisting our children to get better at reading and also help them with their homeworks.

We currently have 23 kids we are helping and are proud to say we are seeing a tremendous progress in them so far.

We cater for kids from grade 1-7 at the moment, and planning to include even higher grades.

And the subjects we currently offer are English and Setswana.
